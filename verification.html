Electrical Subsystem
O-1.1-ELEC-1 Shall provide distinguishable illumination when operating in an ambient-light environment, such that a visual distinction is made when illuminated elements are ON vs OFF in an ambient light (outdoor) environment between sunup and sunset. Demonstration Demonstrate that there is a visual distinction between the OFF state and the PLANTS & PEOPLE state in an ambient light (outdoor environment between sunup and sundown), or brighter (such as a well-lit room). Metric: PASS/FAIL Verification was conducted using two videos. Video O-1.1-ELEC-1-1.mov, recorded outdoors under ambient lighting conditions, shows the system cycling through all three states twice; the OFF and PLANTS & PEOPLE states are visually distinguishable. Video O-1.1-ELEC-1-2.mov, recorded in a well-lit indoor environment at MyFab, shows a transition from the OFF state to the PLANTS & PEOPLE state with clear visual distinction. The requirement is therefore met. PASS. Testing was conducted in both ambient light and artificial light environments. This ensures that the prototype meets the requirement, even if the outdoor environment is not particularly bright at the time of the demonstration, due to weather.

O-3.1-ELEC-1 Shall use meaningful wire colours and a neat layout to ensure ease of debugging and repair. Inspection Inspect the breadboard to confirm that wire colours distinguish LEDs, buttons, and ground connections, and that wires and resistors are arranged in an organized layout, lie flat against the breadboard surface, and have minimal overlap. Metric: PASS/FAIL. Inspection of image O-3.1-ELEC-1.HEIC confirms that wire colours are used to distinguish functional connections: red wires for the red LED circuit, blue wires for the blue LED circuit, green wires for the green LED circuit, white wires for power connections to both pushbuttons, and black wires for ground. The breadboard layout is organized, with wires and resistors lying flat against the breadboard surface and no overlap between components. The requirement is satisfied. PASS. N/A
