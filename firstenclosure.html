First Enclosure Iteration: Design and Integration Issues

The file old_case_fit_issue.mov documents the inability of the breadboard to fully fit within the designed enclosure. This issue arose from two primary factors. First, one of the breadboard support divots was slightly mispositioned, preventing proper insertion of the breadboard into the case. Second, a structural region intended to separate the button interface from the wiring extended too far into the enclosure volume, interfering with the microprocessor. As a result, the breadboard could not sit flush within the case when nearly fully inserted.
