Build Process Overview

This section presents the iterative evolution of the system across its electrical, software, and structural subsystems. Development progressed through repeated cycles of prototyping, testing, and refinement rather than a linear sequence, with findings in each domain directly influencing decisions in the others. Electrical iterations examined component placement, wiring strategy, and user interaction; software revisions incrementally refined control logic and state accessibility through formal finite state modeling; and structural concepts advanced from exploratory sketches to validated CAD and CAM models. Together, the images in this section document critical design decisions and trade-off evaluations, illustrating how constraints, testing outcomes, and iterative feedback guided the system from early concepts to a converged and validated final design.
