Electrical Integration Adjustments

Finally, the resistor value for the blue LED was increased from 220 Ω to 1000 Ω. This change was made after integration revealed that the blue LED was significantly brighter than the green LED, an imbalance that had not been apparent during earlier subsystem testing. Increasing the resistance reduced the current through the blue LED, resulting in a more balanced perceived brightness across all LEDs. The images resistor_before.HEIC and resistor_after.HEIC document the resistor change.
