Final As-Built Prototype

The file final_integrated_prototype.mp4 and final_integrated_prototype.jpg document the completed, as-built prototype system. The video shows the fully assembled electromechanical subsystem, including the enclosure, buttons, and LEDs, followed by integration with the software. The system is then powered and operated to demonstrate correct circuit behavior and successful coordination between the electrical hardware and the programmed control logic. The image shows a static shot of the system in its PLANTS & PEOPLE (All LEDs on) state. This final demonstration confirms that the integrated prototype functions as intended after the design iterations and integration adjustments described in the preceding sections.
