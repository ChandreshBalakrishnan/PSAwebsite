Final As-Built Prototype

The file final_integrated_prototype.mov documents the completed, as-built prototype system. The system is then powered and operated to demonstrate correct circuit behavior and successful coordination between the electrical hardware and the programmed control logic. This final demonstration confirms that the integrated prototype functions as intended after the design iterations and integration adjustments described in the preceding sections.
